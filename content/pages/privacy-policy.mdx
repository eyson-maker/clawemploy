## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ClawEmploy ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our AI agent platform and related services at clawemploy.com.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create a ClawEmploy account, we collect:

- **Email address** and display name
- **Authentication data** from social login providers (Google, GitHub) if used
- **Billing information** processed securely through our payment provider (Creem/Stripe)

### Conversation and Task Data

When you interact with AI agents on our platform, we collect:

- **Agent conversations** — messages you send to and receive from AI agents
- **Agent memory** — context and preferences your agents store to improve task performance
- **Task logs** — records of tasks your agents execute, including inputs, outputs, and status

### GitHub Repository Access

If you connect a GitHub account or grant repository access to an AI agent:

- We access **only the repositories and scopes you explicitly authorize**
- Repository data is used solely for agent task execution (e.g., code review, issue triage)
- We do not store full repository contents beyond what is needed for active tasks

### Usage and Device Data

- **Usage data** — pages visited, features used, agent interactions, and credit consumption
- **Device data** — IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs
- **Analytics data** — aggregated performance metrics collected via Vercel Analytics

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the ClawEmploy platform and AI agent services
- Process credit transactions and manage your subscription
- Execute AI agent tasks on your behalf
- Send transactional emails (account verification, password reset, low-balance alerts)
- Monitor platform usage and enforce daily credit limits
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
- Comply with legal obligations

## AI Agent Data Processing

AI agents on ClawEmploy process your data to perform tasks. This includes:

- **Agent memory** is scoped per agent and per user. Agents retain context from previous interactions to improve performance.
- **Task execution data** is logged for transparency and debugging. You can view task logs in your dashboard.
- **Third-party AI providers** (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Replicate) may process your prompts and task inputs. These providers operate under their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

We do not use your conversation data or task outputs to train AI models.

## Credit Transactions

- We record all credit purchases, allocations, usage, and expiry events.
- Credit transaction history is available in your dashboard.
- Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment provider. We do not store full credit card numbers.

## Data Retention

- **Account data** is retained for as long as your account is active.
- **Agent memory and task logs** are retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.
- **Credit transaction records** are retained for 12 months after account closure for accounting purposes.
- **Analytics data** is retained in aggregated, anonymized form.

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## Your Rights (GDPR and Global Privacy)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Delete** your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Restrict** or **object to** certain processing activities
- **Withdraw consent** where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@clawemploy.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), secure authentication, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate ClawEmploy, including:

- **Authentication providers** (Google, GitHub)
- **Payment processing** (Creem/Stripe)
- **AI model providers** (OpenAI, Anthropic, Replicate)
- **Analytics** (Vercel Analytics)
- **Email delivery** (Resend)

Each provider processes data under their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

ClawEmploy is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the date above.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, contact us at **support@clawemploy.com**.
